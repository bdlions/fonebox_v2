International wholesale voice:

We trading route at an affordable price. Our international voice wholesale service offers direct termination and clarity in voice with maximum uptime. A team of professional monitors the entire process 24x7 to deliver the highest quality to our clients.

Call aggregation:

From our calling card users and business cronies around the globe, we aggregating huge amount of traffic per month.

Pin less calling card and Dialer for all platform:

Making international phone calls is effortless and affordable with Fonebox's Pinless international calling card and dialer. We offer genuine rates for A-Z direct connections with world’s most advanced featured dialer for all mobile and desktop platforms.

Hosted VoIP solution:

We deliver complete solution for VOIP switches with high speed device, maximum uptime and user friendly interface. A team of highly trained professionals observing and developing the system 24x7 to make it more efficient and intelligible for our valuable clients.

Global airtime top-up:

Our global top up offers a unique and professional service that allows communities and roaming subscribers to buy and send airtime for their friends and families back home from any place at any time.

A-Z SMS/MMS routing:

FoneBox is a highly scalable service platform to get the best quality and stable SMS/MMS routes for destinations all over the world at tremendous rates. It supports a vast range of solutions that minimize the costs, maximize the efficiency and the security of SMS delivery.

Switching solution:

Centum softswitch is our next generation VoIP class 4 and class 5 cost-effective switching solution with most advanced VoIP telephony features for retail and enterprise clients.
